Governance Rules

The system enforces monotonic confirmation.
Once a downgrade occurs, it cannot be upgraded in the same run.

Hard stop prevents output emission.
Degradation mode may remove LLM usage.
Cross-check must validate LLM output when enabled.

ExecutionEnvelope restricts allowed actions:
- RUN_LLM
- STOP
- TOOL_CALL (bounded)

ToolEnvelope restricts:
- allowed_tools
- max_calls

Agent decisions are advisory only.
Final authority belongs to orchestrator.
